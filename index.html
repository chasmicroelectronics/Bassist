Chris Tiberius Heald - Bass Player & BV's

Summary

If you want a reliable, solid low-ender, look no further! Chris is a dedicated rock, pop and metal bass player with posh gear and genre-crossing talents.

Equipment

Chris plays a Wood n Tronic Aileron 6 string headless, Shuker Jazz 5 string fretless, a Warwick Thumb 6 string and a Peavey C5 passive (5 string) though a TC electronic Spectra comp, MXR DI+ into a Genz Benz STL9.2 and out of a pair of Vanderkley 1x12 cabs for a tectonic 1000W (but he can also play quietly). All of which fits in his own vehicle, that he can drive all by himself.

Repertoire

Outside of various originals bands, Chris has performed in function bands covering a large number of rock, pop and club standards, etc. From classic rock such as Deep Purple, Whitesnake and Rainbow through to Muse and Audioslave on the modern side and the likes of Cher, Patti Smith. Tina Turner, Toto in the pop genre
